02 · statement
Folio · the four cuts

Four research moves that kept conclusions sharp.

Trades volume for force.

Plate group A · cf. plates §07–§10
§ 01

Frame, then read.

Brief first. Read only to test a stated claim.

§ 02

One interview, one decision.

Retire one possibility per session. Name the decision before the call.

§ 03

Source the disagreement.

Cite the strongest counter-evidence by name, or the question isn't ready.

§ 04

Print, don't archive.

If findings won't fit a folio page, they weren't structured to ship.

Plate book

How a small team built the 8-week research cadence.

i.

Brief on Monday.

Week 01 · scoping

One page: the question, the disagreement, the decision by week 8.

ii.

Read on a clock.

Weeks 02–03 · corpus

A 9-hour reading window. Marked sources, never highlights.

iii.

Interview to retire.

Weeks 04–06 · field

Each interview retires one possibility from a short-list.

iv.

Print the folio.

Weeks 07–08 · publish

Deliverable: a four-page folio: claim, evidence, counter, decision.
